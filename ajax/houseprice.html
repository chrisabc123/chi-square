Project Info:

This project was carried out as a data field practice assignment of Korea Data Agency in 2019, and we predicted soaring house prices.

At that time, thinking that house prices were bubbles in 2019, the Planck blackbody radiation spectrum was used as a way to numerically represent Minsky's bubble economy model. The reason for using the Planck distribution is that it is easy to fit data using six parameters with a shape similar to the Minsky model.

For the analysis method, 5 out of 6 parameters of the Planck blackbody radiation spectrum were fixed by fitting to one data set, and the T parameter was used as a variable representing the size of bubble.

As a project that took place in the summer of 2019, a decrease was predicted after a 1.5-fold increase at the time. The average sales price in the summer of 2019 was 1589466000 Won to 2311539000 Won in 2022, showing a decreasing trend after an increase of exactly 1.5 times within the error range.
